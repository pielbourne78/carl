Privacy Policy

At ROUNDZ, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Such as name, email address, and contact details when you register or place an order.
  • Payment Information: Such as credit card or payment details for transaction processing.
  • Usage Information: Including pages viewed, interaction data, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process orders and deliver products
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send notifications about your orders or important updates
  • To personalize your shopping experience
  • To send promotional offers, if you opt-in

3. How We Protect Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. This includes encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to data.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except as necessary to fulfill your requests, such as processing payments or shipping orders. We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website and services, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data, where applicable
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

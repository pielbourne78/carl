Payment Options

At ROUNDZ, we offer a range of secure and convenient payment methods to enhance your shopping experience. Select the option that best suits your needs.

1. Credit & Debit Cards

We accept major credit and debit cards, including:

  • Visa
  • Mastercard
  • American Express
  • Discover

Payments made via credit or debit card are processed securely, and we do not store your card information.

2. PayPal

With PayPal, you can securely link your bank account, debit, or credit card to make payments directly without entering your card details.

Simply choose PayPal at checkout and log into your account to complete the transaction.

3. Apple Pay

If youâ€™re using an Apple device, enjoy a fast and secure payment experience with Apple Pay. Select Apple Pay at checkout and confirm the payment using Face ID or Touch ID.

4. Google Pay

Google Pay is available for Android users. With your payment details securely stored, Google Pay provides a quick checkout process for a seamless shopping experience.

5. Bank Transfer

For larger transactions, you may prefer a direct bank transfer. Please contact our customer service team to arrange this option and receive the necessary account details.

6. Gift Cards

ROUNDZ gift cards can be redeemed during checkout. Simply enter your gift card code, and the balance will be applied to your purchase.

If you have questions or require assistance with payment, please contact us at Customer Support.
